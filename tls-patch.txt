Adds support for delegating TLS certificate provisioning and/or peer
verification to an external shared library (.so). The plugin is
activated solely by setting –tls-plugin-so (plus at least one hook
flag); absence of those flags leaves all existing behaviour unchanged.

–tls-plugin-verify-cert set => ca.crt not required on disk
